Who's behind soul?

Hamze Elmi

I first started making concepts of soul in 2016. I was using those free design websites and let's just say I wouldn't see anyone wearing those designs. I didn't give up though. I kept thinking of new ideas and kept experimenting. It was only until September 2018 that I was given the opportunity to make this idea come to life.

Right now, most of my time is spent being a computer science student at Carleton University. However, when I am not drowning in homework, I like to write. I like to switch up the forms of writing but I mostly stick to spoken word. My poetry page is @zeedoespoetry on Instagram. I'm also big on helping and participating in my community.It's because of my community that I am the person I am today, so I think it's only right I help in any way I can.
